Chloramine Stability Tool (CaST)

v2022.02

Chloramine Stability Tool (CaST®) can be used to manage chloramine effectively in a reservoir (service tank) CasT®:

  • uses easily measurable parameters such as chlorine, ammonia and temperature
  • assess the current chloramine stability and nitrification risk
  • predicts the future chloramine stability and nitrification risk
  • helps evaluate the results of various interventions to stabilise chloramine residual

Developed by Prof. Arumugam Sathasivan, Western Sydney University, Kingswood, NSW, Australia.
